About

Crimson Crescent Publishing LLC is an independent publisher of dark fantasy fiction, established in 2026 and registered in Illinois.

We publish thoughtful speculative fiction for readers who linger in the shadows. Our focus is stories built on deep worlds, morally complex characters, and atmosphere that earns its dark.

Our founder is author and digital technologist David A. Graham, writing as D.A. Graham.
